Pokemon Fangame Projects

Now you may be asking youself, what exactly they are? A Fangame simply is a game based on a series that you enjoy and try to emulate the style of.
Though to the Pokemon Community it's a bit different, in this community there are two types of games you can make, ROM Hacks or Fangames.

A ROM Hack takes and edits the original code of the ROM used to build the new experience around what is present, and with the big benefit of being playable on original systems!

A Fangame makes use of a 3rd party program, mainly RPGMaker XP. One downside of the games being made on a different program means it won't be playable on original systems like ROMs can.
